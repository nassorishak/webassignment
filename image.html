Coffee is a popular beverage made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of the Coffea plant. It is typically brewed by steeping the ground coffee beans in hot water, and it can be consumed hot or cold, with or without added milk, cream, sugar, or flavorings. Coffee contains caffeine, a natural stimulant that can help to increase alertness and reduce fatigue. It also contains antioxidants, which can help to protect the body against damage from harmful molecules called free radicals. However, excessive consumption of coffee can have negative effects on health, such as insomnia, anxiety, and digestive problems.
